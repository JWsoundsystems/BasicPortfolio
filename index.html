About Me

Technical Support Associate
Meyer Sound Laboratroies, Inc.



I provide product support for Meyer Sound’s catalog of professional audio equipment, including digital signal processing units, loudspeakers and electronics. My Primary focus is to provide customers with pre-configured and tested digital systems prior to shipment.

I support customers remotely and on job sites when required and work with customers on planning during all phases of system design/installation, as well as support rental systems and tours.

I work closely with Meyer Sound’s Berkeley office, other Technical Support representatives globally, Sales Managers and distributors in numerous global territories where Meyer Sound products are sold.
